About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the game. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, Kohli made his ODI debut in 2008 and has since amassed numerous records and accolades.

Career Highlights

  • Scored over 75 international centuries across formats
  • Led India to the top of the Test rankings
  • Fastest player to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in ODIs
  • Winner of ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times

Legacy

Known for his intensity, fitness, and consistency, Kohli has inspired a generation of cricketers with his passion and dedication to the game.
